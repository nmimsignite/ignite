ABOUT ABOUT ABOUT ABOUT ABOUT ABOUT ABOUT
IGNITE is the annual technical festival of SVKM's NMIMS, Navi Mumbai. We started in the year 2019 with an aim to provide the platform for the technologies of the future while into a festival of amusement and illuminations for a better tomorrow.

Last year, the fest witnessed more than a dozen incredible events as well as live performances by famous standup comedians Gaurav Kapoor and Joel Dsouza and this year we bring you IGNITE with all new events and formats right into yours homes.
